My favourite movies

Below are three movies that one should watch once in their lifetime

Over the hedge

One of the best animated movies with a great cast of voice actors. A movie that will make you come back to watch it again.

Kung fu Hustle

An action packed comedy movie with one of the best vfx at the time. Incredible story and a wide range of characters.

Interstellar

Best sci fi movie ever. The music elevates the experience for the viewer.
